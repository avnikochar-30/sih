PM‑KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

Direct income support to small and marginal farmers — pmkisan.gov.in

  • What: Periodic cash transfers to eligible farmer families.
  • Eligibility: Small & marginal farmers; Aadhaar, land records; excludes certain high‐income govt employees. Check portal.
  • How to apply / status check: Via PMKISAN portal or nearest CSC (Common Service Centre).

PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)

Crop insurance covering yield loss, weather events & post‐harvest risks — pmfby.gov.in

  • What: Insured coverage for notified crops/seasons/areas.
  • Eligibility: Both loanee & non‐loanee farmers in notified areas; see guidelines for crops/seasons.
  • How to claim: Via insurer, CSCs or mandi offices; follow timelines on portal.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Short‑term crop loans & working capital support — see SBI & bank pages. SBI KCC

  • What: Credit limits to cover crop/pre‑harvest/post‑harvest expenses & allied activities.
  • Eligibility: Farmers with cultivable land; depends on bank; may need prior credit history.
  • How to apply: Through bank branches or CSC; some banks have online forms.

Soil Health Card (SHC)

Soil testing with nutrient and fertilizer advisories — soilhealth.dac.gov.in

  • What: Lab soil test, printed advisory card with crop‐wise nutrient/dose suggestions.
  • Eligibility: All farmers with land; contact state agriculture / SHC centres.
  • How to use: Use recommendations while planning fertilizer; some subsidy schemes link to this.

e‑NAM (National Agriculture Market)

Online mandi & price discovery platform — enam.gov.in

  • What: Platform that connects multiple mandis for better market access & fair pricing.
  • Eligibility: Farmers, traders, buyers registered on e‑NAM.
  • How to register: Through e‑NAM portal or via mandi officials enrolling producers.

Per Drop More Crop (PDMC, PMKSY component)

Support for drip & sprinkler systems to increase water‐use efficiency — pmksy.gov.in

  • What: Subsidy & technical support for micro‑irrigation.
  • Eligibility: Individuals, FPOs, farmer groups; state‐specific guides apply.
  • How to apply: Through state agriculture dept. or PMKSY portal during scheme windows.

Last verified on official portals. For state‑wise variances & latest updates, consult your State Agriculture Department or nearest CSC.
